Week

Understanding Your Anxiety

01
"You're not broken — your alarm system is just a little too loud. This week, you find out why."

What's Actually Happening — Anxiety is your body's alert system, doing exactly what it evolved to do — just a little overactive for the modern world you're living in. Understanding this reframes everything that follows: you're not fixing something broken, you're recalibrating something oversensitive.

"You are not your anxiety. You are the one noticing it — and that noticing is where all your power lives."
This Week's Practice

Read Chapters 1–2 of your companion Guide · Start your Daily Tracker · Notice one moment today where anxiety showed up, and simply name it.
